Popular Authors

The wizards of words.

Ana Huang

Ana Huang is a popular American author known for her contemporary romance novels, particularly her Twisted series.

Leigh Bardugo

Leigh Bardugo is a critically acclaimed American author best known for her Grishaverse series, which includes the Shadow and Bone trilogy and Six of Crows duology.

Alex Michaelides

Alex Michaelides is a British author known for his psychological thrillers. His debut novel, The Silent Patient, was a worldwide bestseller and has been adapted into a film.
