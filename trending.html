POPULAR BOOKS OF 2024 SO FAR

2024 has been a year of bookish adventures! From heart-pounding thrillers to heartwarming romances, there's been something for every reader. We and many other readers have been hooked on certain books this year, lost in their pages, and eagerly awaiting the next chapter. With so many amazing stories to choose from, it's no wonder we're already planning our 2025 reading list. So, grab your favorite beverage, cozy up with a good book, and get ready for another year of literary bliss!

Lets have a look at BOOK NOOK's most popular books of the year:-
Fiction:
"The Midnight Library" by Matt Haig - A heartwarming exploration of life's choices and regrets.
"Pachinko" by Min Jin Lee - A sweeping epic following a Korean family through generations.
"The Vanishing Half" by Brit Bennett - A captivating story of twins who take vastly different paths in life.
Non-Fiction:
"Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind" by Yuval Noah Harari - A groundbreaking exploration of human history and evolution.
"The Body Keeps the Score" by Bessel van der Kolk - A groundbreaking exploration of trauma and healing.
"Educated" by Tara Westover - A memoir of a young woman's extraordinary journey from isolation to education.
